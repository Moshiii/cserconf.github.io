Top ten(ish) recommended books and papers

Top 10 ( or there about ) Books / Papers recommended by and for SCAMers.

  1. "The Psychology of Computer Programming", Silver Anniversary Edition by Gerald M. Weinberg. ISBN: 0932633420.
  2. ENGLER et al. "Bugs as Deviant Behavior".
  3. "An Introduction to General Systems Thinking", by Gerald M. Weinberg. ISBN: 0932633498.
  4. Aho, Seti, Ullman, "Compiler Construction", "The Dragon book". ( or Fischer and LaBlanc ).
  5. "Program Slicing", Weiser 1982 and 1984.
  6. "Interprocedural slicing using dependence graphs." ACM Transactions on Programming Languages and Systems 12, 1 (January 1990), pp. 26-60.
  7. Algebra I ( V.D. Liaerden)
