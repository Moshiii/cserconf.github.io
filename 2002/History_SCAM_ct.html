History of SCAM

The first SCAM workshop (SCAM 2001) was held in Florence, on November 10th 2001, co-located with ICSM 2001, WESS 2001and WSE 2001. The workshop was a one day event with the emphasis on discussion. 48 people attended the workshop. The SCAM 2001 Proceedings are published by the IEEE and there will be a special issue of the journal Information and Software Technology to appear shortly featuring extended versions of selected papers from SCAM 2001.
