Location

SCAM 2002 took place at CRIM (Centre de Recherche Informatique de Montréal). CRIM is located in Montréal downtown. The address is 550 Sherbrooke Street West, 1st floor, Montréal (QC) Canada, H3A 1B9. . This is just a very short walk from the ICSM conference hotel (The Holiday Inn), which is at 420 Sherbrooke Street West.
