Please remember that you’ll need to get a visa well in advance of your trip.

General visa requirements include a completed visa application form, two 5 cm x 5 cm (2 inch x 2 inch) full-faced passport photos, an actual passport with at least two blank pages (valid for at least 6 months from the date of travel), conference confirmation letter, and payment for the visa fee. Please allow enough time for processing.
