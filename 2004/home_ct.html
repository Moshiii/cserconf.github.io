Important note on Deadlines for SCAM 2004

The deadline of April 5th 2004 is firm for abstracts; the complete paper must be received by April 12th 2004 at the very latest. Papers submitted later than April 5th for which we have not received an abstract by April 5th cannot be considered.

Deadlines are expressed in Apia time. You might want to consult the World Clock.
