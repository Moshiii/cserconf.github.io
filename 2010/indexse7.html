Venue

SCAM2010 is co-located with ICSM2010 hosted by the city of Timişoara , the second largest (around 312,000 inhabitants) and probably the most cosmopolitan city of Romania.

How to get here and Stay.

The venue is the building of the Faculty for Automation and Computer Engineering, located very close to Timişoara’s historical center (7 min. walk), in a space full of parks that guard the Bega river’s main channel. More information please visit http://icsm2010.upt.ro/home/venue.
